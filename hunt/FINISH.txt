================================  FINISH  ======================================

Say the six words in order. That is the location password.

To claim your prize:
  1. Write the full phrase down.
  2. Submit it where your instructor tells you (or show a TA).

One last trick — look at the whole map you just walked, in one command:

    lt

(`lt` shows a 2-level tree; `lt -L 3` goes deeper.) Every wrong turn you made
is a folder you now know how to escape. That was the point.
